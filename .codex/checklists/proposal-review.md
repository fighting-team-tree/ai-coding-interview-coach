# Proposal Review Checklist

Use this before finalizing proposal-oriented output.

- Does the document clearly position the product as a bridge from coding test performance to interview readiness?
- Is the core user journey continuous from code submission to mock interview to feedback?
- Is hallucination control described as a concrete architectural mechanism rather than a vague promise?
- Are the three evaluation axes visible somewhere in the product or system story?
- Do the technical claims sound plausible for a long-horizon competition rather than a one-off hackathon demo?
- Does the business framing extend beyond a weak B2C-only story?
- Are legal, safety, cheating, and cost risks at least acknowledged where relevant?
- Is the content mapped to `docs/proposal_template.md` rather than an invented structure?
- Are HWP template helper texts or example fillers excluded from the final output?
- Are speculative future capabilities labeled as roadmap items instead of current implementation facts?
